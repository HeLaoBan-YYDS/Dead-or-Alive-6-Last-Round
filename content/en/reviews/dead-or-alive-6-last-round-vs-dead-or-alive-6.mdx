## FAQ: Dead or Alive 6 Last Round vs Dead or Alive 6

### 1. Does Dead or Alive 6 Last Round include all previous DLC?
While it includes the five additional characters that were released as DLC for the original game, it does not include every single costume pack. Most previously purchased costumes can be transferred if you own them on the same account, but collaboration costumes (like those from SNK) are generally excluded from the transfer process.

### 2. Is there crossplay between the original DOA6 and Last Round?
No, there is no crossplay between **dead or alive 6 last round vs dead or alive 6**. Players on the original 2019 version will not be able to match with those playing the 2026 Last Round edition. This is largely due to the differences in the lighting engine and technical optimizations that affect frame data and synchronization.

### 3. Who is the new character in DOA6 Last Round?
As of June 25, 2026, the identity of the new character has not been revealed. Series producer Yosuke Hayashi confirmed that a completely new fighter was developed alongside the game to revitalize the community, with a formal reveal expected in a future update.

### 4. Can I transfer my rank from the original game?
Unfortunately, ranking points, fighter levels, and player points do not carry over. When moving from **dead or alive 6 last round vs dead or alive 6**, you will start fresh in the competitive rankings. However, your story progress, DOA Quest completions, and unlocked titles (excluding collaboration titles) will transfer over smoothly.
