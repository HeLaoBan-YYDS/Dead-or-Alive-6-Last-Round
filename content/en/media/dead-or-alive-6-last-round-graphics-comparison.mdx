## Performance Optimization Guide for Players

If you are playing on PC or looking to tweak your console settings for the best competitive edge, visual quality is only half the battle. High frame rates and low input lag are critical in fighting games where frames are measured in milliseconds. 

Here are some actionable tips to optimize your setup:
* **Enable Game Mode:** Ensure your TV or monitor has Game Mode enabled to reduce input lag.
* **Prioritize Framerate:** In the game settings, always choose the "Prioritize Framerate" option over "Prioritize Resolution" if your hardware struggles to maintain a locked 60 FPS.
* **Disable V-Sync on PC:** If you are using a G-Sync or FreeSync monitor, disable in-game V-Sync to reduce frame delay.
* **Set Texture Filtering to Max:** Anisotropic filtering has a very low performance cost on modern GPUs but makes ground textures look significantly sharper.
