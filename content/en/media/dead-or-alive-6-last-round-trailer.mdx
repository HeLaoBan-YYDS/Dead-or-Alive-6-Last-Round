## Core Fighters, Save Transfers, and Product Information

To ensure that the community remains active, Koei Tecmo has launched a free-to-play version alongside the main release. Known as *Core Fighters*, this version allows players to experience the core gameplay mechanics, practice modes, and select online features without paying upfront. Players can purchase individual character unlock keys as they go.

For players who spent hundreds of hours on the original version, Team NINJA has implemented a comprehensive save data and DLC transfer system. Most cosmetic items, title unlocks, and story mode progress can be migrated to the new version within the same console family. The official [Team NINJA Dead or Alive 6 Last Round website](https://teamninja-studio.com/doa6/lastround/us/) provides complete instructions on how to migrate your purchases.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where can I watch the official dead or alive 6 last round trailer?
The official trailer can be viewed on the Koei Tecmo America YouTube channel or directly on the game's official website. It showcases the launch gameplay, next-gen upgrades, and the new Photo Mode.

### Can I transfer my DLC from the original version to Last Round?
Yes, players can transfer their previously purchased DLC and saved data within the same console family (e.g., PlayStation 4 to PlayStation 5, or Xbox One to Xbox Series X|S). Detailed transfer guides are available on the official website.

### Is there a physical disc release for this edition?
No, this current-gen upgrade is available as a digital download only. You can purchase it directly from the PlayStation Store, Xbox Games Store, or Steam.

### What is included in the free-to-play Core Fighters version?
The *Core Fighters* version includes access to four playable characters, basic training modes, and online multiplayer matches. Additional characters and the Story Mode must be purchased separately.
