## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I transfer my dead or alive 6 last round all costumes across different platforms?
No, DLC and save data transfers are restricted to the same account and platform family. For example, you can transfer from PlayStation 4 to PlayStation 5, or from one PC to another via Steam, but you cannot move your costumes from Xbox to PlayStation or vice versa.

### Why didn't my Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond costumes transfer?
Costumes for collaboration characters like Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond are subject to specific licensing agreements. According to the official product info, these specific DLC items cannot be carried over to the Last Round version, and players may need to re-purchase the character unlock keys to access them in the new game.

### What happens to my Premium Tickets in Dead or Alive 6 Last Round?
Premium Tickets do not carry over. If you have unused tickets in the PlayStation 4 version of Dead or Alive 6, it is recommended that you spend them on costumes before initiating the transfer, as the tickets themselves will not appear in your Last Round inventory.

### Is there crossplay between the original DOA6 and Last Round?
No, there is no crossplay support between Dead or Alive 6 and Dead or Alive 6 Last Round. Players on the new version will only be able to compete against others who have also upgraded to the Last Round edition.
