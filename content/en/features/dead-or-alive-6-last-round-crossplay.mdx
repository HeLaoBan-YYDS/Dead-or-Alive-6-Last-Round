Players on PlayStation 5 cannot match against those on Xbox Series X|S or Steam. Furthermore, there is no crossplay between the original DEAD OR ALIVE 6 and the newer DEAD OR ALIVE 6 Last Round. Because **dead or alive 6 last round crossplay** is not supported, you must ensure your friends are on the exact same platform and game version to play together.

## How to Transfer DLC Costumes to Last Round

While we won't see **dead or alive 6 last round crossplay** implemented, the developers did provide robust local data migration tools. If you have spent real money on cosmetic packs in the original game, you can carry most of them forward. Note that DLC can only be transferred between the exact same account on the respective network (PlayStation Network, Xbox Network, or Steam). You cannot transfer DLC across different platforms, such as moving PlayStation purchases to Steam.

For PlayStation 5 players, you must first update your PlayStation 4 copy of the base game to Version 1.23. Once updated, launch the base game, head to the Main Menu, and upload your DLC data. After this step is complete, sign into the same account on your PlayStation 5, boot up Last Round, and your content will automatically sync. If you no longer own the physical PS4 disc, player experiences suggest downloading the free-to-play *Core Fighters* version to upload your data instead.

On Xbox Series X|S and Steam, the process is slightly more automated but requires attention. On Xbox, you must physically install your original DLC packages onto your console storage before launching Last Round. Once installed, the game will recognize and unlock them.

| Platform | Key Requirement Before Transfer | Transfer Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PlayStation 5** | Update PS4 version to Ver. 1.23 | Upload DLC data from PS4 Main Menu, then launch Last Round |
| **Xbox Series X|S** | Install original DLC files on the console | Launch Last Round; DLC transfers automatically |
| **Steam (PC)** | Log into the same Steam account | Launch Last Round; DLC transfers automatically |

## Transferring Save Data and Progression

Migrating your save data is another critical step when moving to the Last Round edition. The game features an automatic detection system that prompts you to import your progress upon reaching the title screen. However, you must be careful: if you have already created a brand-new save file in Last Round, the transfer prompt will not appear. You must delete any existing Last Round save data to trigger the migration prompt.

For PlayStation users, transferring data on the same console is seamless. If you are moving to a new PlayStation 5 console, you will need to utilize PlayStation Plus cloud storage or a USB drive to copy your PS4 save file over first. Xbox players benefit from the Xbox Network cloud save system, which automatically syncs your progress as long as you are signed into the same profile.

Steam players can easily transfer their files using Steam Cloud. Alternatively, you can manually copy your save folder from your old PC to your new one. The default directory for manual transfers is:

`\Users\(Your Username)\Documents\KoeiTecmo\DEADORALIVE6`

| Xbox One / Series X|S | Xbox Series X|S | Xbox Network Cloud Sync |

## Save Data Carry-Over Status: What Transfers?

Not every piece of data transfers cleanly between the two editions. While most cosmetic unlocks and single-player progress will carry over, competitive online statistics and rankings do not. This means your leaderboard rankings, Fighter Points, and online records will reset when you start playing Last Round.

Additionally, certain collaboration characters and their associated DLC items are restricted due to licensing agreements. Specifically, the popular guest characters Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond cannot be carried over automatically. You will need to acquire separate unlock keys to access them in the new edition.

The following table details exactly what items and progression milestones will carry over to your new game:

| Data Type | Specific Item | Carry-Over Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cosmetics** | Standard Costumes, Hairstyles, Glasses | **Yes** (Requires character unlock key) |
| **Cosmetics** | Collaboration Costumes (Mai & Kula) | **No** (Licensing restrictions) |
| **Currency** | Premium Tickets | **No** (Cannot be transferred) |
| **Single Player** | Story Mode, DOA Quest, Training Progress | **Yes** (Full progression carries over) |
| **User Profile** | Ranking, Fighter Level, Fight Records | **No** (Reset to zero) |
| **Settings** | Option Configurations, Closet Sets, Replays | **No** (Must be set up again) |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there any workaround for the lack of dead or alive 6 last round crossplay?
No, there is no workaround or mod that enables cross-platform play. Matchmaking is strictly limited to players on the same platform family (PlayStation to PlayStation, Xbox to Xbox, and Steam to Steam) who are running the Last Round edition.

### Can I play with someone who owns the standard version of Dead or Alive 6?
No. Unfortunately, the absence of **dead or alive 6 last round crossplay** also extends to matchmaking between the base game and the Last Round edition. Both players must be playing the exact same edition on the same platform to connect online.

### Why didn't my Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond DLC transfer?
Due to licensing agreements with SNK, collaboration content like Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond does not carry over to the Last Round edition automatically. You will need to purchase the specific unlock keys for these characters again on the new edition's store page.

### What should I do if the save transfer prompt does not appear?
If the game does not ask you to transfer your data upon reaching the title screen, it is likely because you have already generated a save file for Last Round. To fix this, close the game, delete the Last Round save file from your system storage, and restart the game.
