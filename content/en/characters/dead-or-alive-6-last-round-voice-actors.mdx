## Key Casting Changes and Behind-the-Scenes Shifts

The transition to this generation of the game brought several significant changes to the English voice cast. Localization teams face unique challenges, including union regulations, scheduling conflicts, and creative direction changes. For this title, the selection of **dead or alive 6 last round voice actors** represents a major shift in how key characters sound.

### The Return of Janice Kawaye as Ayane

One of the most discussed shifts in the community is the casting of Janice Kawaye as Ayane. Kawaye is a highly respected voice actress known for her work in various anime and video games. 

While she has previously voiced Ayane in the *Ninja Gaiden* spin-off series and *Fatal Frame: Maiden of Black Water*, this game marks the first time she voices the character in a mainline *Dead or Alive* entry. She replaces Brittney Lee Harvey, who voiced Ayane in *Dead or Alive 5*. Community feedback suggests that Kawaye brings a darker, more experienced tone to the character that fits her ninja background perfectly.

### Dave B. Mitchell Steps in as Ryu Hayabusa

Another major casting adjustment involves the legendary Dragon Ninja, Ryu Hayabusa. In previous titles, Ryu was voiced by the prolific Troy Baker. However, because this project was produced under a non-union contract, Baker (who is a union actor) could not reprise the role. 

As a result, Dave B. Mitchell stepped in to voice the character. Mitchell is no stranger to the franchise, having voiced Ryu in *Dead or Alive: Dimensions* all the way through to *Yaiba: Ninja Gaiden Z*. His reprisal of the role brings a sense of familiar authority to the legendary ninja.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who are the primary dead or alive 6 last round voice actors for the main characters?

### Why did Troy Baker not return to voice Ryu Hayabusa?
Troy Baker did not return to voice Ryu Hayabusa because the game was produced under a non-union contract. Since Baker is a union actor, he was unable to take part in the project. Dave B. Mitchell, who has a long history with the character, stepped in to voice Ryu instead.

No, guest characters from *The King of Fighters* franchise, such as Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond, do not have English voice actors in the dub. They retain their original Japanese voice tracks across all versions of the game.

### Is Tamaki voiced in English in Dead or Alive 6: Last Round?
Tamaki is the only non-guest *Dead or Alive* character who does not feature an English voice track in the game. Players will hear her Japanese voice regardless of the chosen language settings.
