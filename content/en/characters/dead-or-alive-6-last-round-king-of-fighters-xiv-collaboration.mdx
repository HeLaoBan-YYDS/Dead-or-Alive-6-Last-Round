## Combat Strategies for SNK Characters in a 3D Space

According to community reports, adapting to the **dead or alive 6 last round king of fighters xiv collaboration** mechanics requires adjusting your spatial awareness to account for classic SNK zoning tools. In a traditional 2D fighting game, jumping or blocking are your primary defenses against projectiles. In Dead or Alive 6, the addition of the lateral sidestep maneuver completely changes the dynamic of projectile play.

When playing as Mai, use your Kachosen (fan toss) to bait your opponent into sidestepping. Once they commit to a lateral movement, punish them with a tracking strike like her Ryuuenbu to catch them off guard.

When playing as Kula, leverage her sliding attacks to slip under high-level strikes and projectiles. Her ability to freeze opponents opens up opportunities for devastating close-range command throws and air juggles.
