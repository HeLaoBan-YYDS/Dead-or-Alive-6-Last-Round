## Actionable Tips for Competitive Play on PC

To dominate the online leaderboards, players must adapt to the unique pacing of the game. The franchise is built on the "Triangle System," a rock-paper-scissors mechanic where strikes beat throws, throws beat holds, and holds beat strikes. Understanding this flow is the key to climbing the ranks.

Here are several practical tips for maximizing your performance:

*   **Optimize Input Delay**: Ensure your monitor is set to its highest refresh rate and disable V-Sync in the graphics menu to minimize input lag.
*   **Master the Hold System**: Spend time in training mode learning the distinct animations for high, mid, and low strikes to execute precise counter-holds.
*   **Utilize Danger Zones**: Position your opponent near explosive walls or breakable barriers to maximize your combo damage.
*   **Configure Custom Button Layouts**: Map the Break Gauge activation to a single button for faster reaction times during high-pressure situations.
