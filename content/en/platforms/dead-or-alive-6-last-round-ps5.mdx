## Master the Combat: The Triangle System and Mechanics

Whether you are a newcomer or a seasoned veteran, mastering combat in dead or alive 6 last round ps5 requires a solid grasp of the game's core systems. The franchise is famous for its **Triangle System**, a rock-paper-scissors style of combat where different move categories counter one another:
1. **Strikes beat Throws:** Fast attacks will interrupt an opponent trying to grab you.
2. **Throws beat Holds:** Grabs easily bypass defensive counter-holds.
3. **Holds beat Strikes:** Defensive counters can deflect and punish incoming strikes.

When you land an attack that successfully counters your opponent's move choice, it triggers a **Hi Counter**, dealing devastating bonus damage. 

Additionally, players must manage the **Break Gauge**. This resource bar allows you to execute powerful moves like the **Break Blow** (a crushing offensive special) and the **Break Hold** (the ultimate defensive escape). For beginners, the **Fatal Rush** mechanic allows you to mash a single button to execute a highly satisfying, easy-to-use combo string.

| Combat Mechanic | Input / Trigger | Strategic Counter / Purpose | Combat Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strikes** | Standard Punch/Kick buttons | Interrupts and beats Throws | Basic damage, starts combos |
| **Throws** | Throw button/command | Punishes and beats defensive Holds | High-damage unblockable grabs |
| **Holds** | Direction + Guard button | Deflects and beats incoming Strikes | Counters opponent strikes, reverses momentum |
| **Fatal Rush** | Mashing the Special button | Easy combo string for beginners | Highly satisfying, accessible combo sequence |
| **Break Blow** | Forward + Special (with full Break Gauge) | Powerful offensive special move | Deals massive damage and breaks through guards |
| **Break Hold** | Back + Special (requires half Break Gauge) | Ultimate defensive counter | Escapes any strike state, regardless of high/mid/low |
