## Step-by-Step: How to Transfer Your Data

The process for moving your **dead or alive 6 last round all dlc** and save data varies slightly depending on your platform. Team NINJA has streamlined this to ensure that your "DOA Quest" progress, story unlocks, and encyclopedia entries aren't lost.

### For PlayStation 5 Users
1. Ensure your PS4 version of Dead or Alive 6 is updated to at least Version 1.23.
2. Launch the PS4 version and navigate to the Main Menu while signed into the PlayStation Network.
3. Select the option to upload your DLC and Save Data to the cloud.
4. Launch Dead or Alive 6 Last Round on your PS5.
5. The game will automatically detect the uploaded data and prompt you to "Transfer."

### For Xbox Series X|S Users
The Xbox ecosystem handles this through the Smart Delivery and Xbox Network systems. As long as you are using the same Microsoft account, your DLC should appear in your "Ready to Install" list. You must install the DLC for the original game before it becomes active in Last Round.

### For Steam (PC) Users
1. Ensure Steam Cloud is enabled in your Steam client settings.
2. Launch the original DOA6 to ensure your latest save is synced.
3. Launch Last Round on the same PC.
4. The game will detect the local save folder (usually located in `Documents\KoeiTecmo\DEADORALIVE6`) and ask to migrate the data.

## FAQ

### Can I transfer my dead or alive 6 last round all dlc from PS4 to Xbox?
No. DLC and save data transfers are only supported within the same console family (PlayStation to PlayStation, Xbox to Xbox, or Steam to Steam). There is no cross-platform transfer or crossplay functionality available at this time.

### Why didn't my Mai Shiranui or Kula Diamond DLC transfer?
Due to the specific licensing agreements with SNK, these collaboration characters are handled differently. While the standard roster of 29 characters in Last Round includes previous DLC fighters like Nyotengu and Phase 4, the SNK characters require their own specific unlock keys and do not support automatic data carry-over for their costumes.

### Is there a free-to-play version of Last Round?
Yes, a "Core Fighters" version is available. It features a limited roster (Kasumi, Marie Rose, Honoka, and NiCO) but allows you to transfer your previously purchased **dead or alive 6 last round all dlc** costumes if you owned them on the same account.

### Does the game support the PS5 DualSense features?
According to producer interviews, the game does not currently utilize specific DualSense haptic feedback or adaptive trigger modes. The focus of the "Last Round" enhancement was primarily on visual fidelity, lighting, and performance stability.
