### Can I play Dead or Alive 6 for free?
Yes. The "Core Fighters" edition is completely free to play on Steam, PlayStation, and Xbox. It features four playable characters (Kasumi, Hitomi, Diego, and Bass) and provides full access to online ranked matches, training modes, and local versus play.

### Does the premium edition include all DLC costumes?
No. Due to the massive volume of cosmetic items and seasonal collaboration outfits, no single edition includes every piece of DLC. The premium editions include select character sets and deluxe costumes, but additional cosmetic packs must be purchased separately.

### Is the story mode included in the free-to-play version?
No, the main Story Mode is locked in the free Core Fighters version. To experience the full narrative campaign, you will need to purchase either the standard game or the Story Mode unlock key as a separate add-on.
