## Understanding the Core Fighters Philosophy

The fighting game landscape has shifted significantly over the last decade, moving toward models that lower the barrier to entry. Team Ninja’s approach with this version is to provide a "living demo" that is actually a fully functional competitive platform. Unlike traditional demos that might limit you to a single stage or a 10-minute timer, this version allows you to engage in the core mechanics of the game indefinitely.

This digital-only release is designed for the modern era, available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and Steam. It focuses on the essential "Core" of the experience: the tight, responsive combat and the famous Triangle System (Strikes beat Throws, Throws beat Holds, and Holds beat Strikes). By offering a curated selection of characters, the developers allow players to master the fundamentals without being overwhelmed by a massive roster from day one.

The DOA community is generally welcoming to "Core Fighters" players, as they provide a healthy population for the online matchmaking pools. Based on player experience, here are a few tips to maximize your time with the free version:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I play against friends who own the full version of the game?
Yes! Players using **dead or alive 6 last round core fighters** can play seamlessly with those who own the standard version in both Ranked and Lobby matches. There are no "split" servers; the community remains unified regardless of which version you are using.

### Do my progress and unlocks carry over if I buy the full game later?
Absolutely. Any costumes you earn, titles you unlock, or Character Keys you purchase in the free-to-play version will carry over if you decide to upgrade to the full standard version of the game. Your online rank and win/loss record will also remain intact.

### Is an internet connection required to play Core Fighters?
While you can play offline modes like Arcade, Training, and local Versus without an internet connection, you will need to be online to access Ranked Matches, Lobby Matches, and to purchase or verify any DLC Unlock Keys you have acquired.

### Which characters are included for free in dead or alive 6 last round core fighters?
The initial free roster includes four characters: Kasumi, Marie Rose, Honoka, and NiCO. All other characters, including guest characters like Mai Shiranui or Kula Diamond, require the purchase of a separate Character Unlock Key or the full version of the game.
