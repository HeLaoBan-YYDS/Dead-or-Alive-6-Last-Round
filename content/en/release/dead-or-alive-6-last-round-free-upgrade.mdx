## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the dead or alive 6 last round free upgrade include the collaboration characters?
No, the upgrade does not automatically include Mai Shiranui or Kula Diamond. Due to licensing agreements with SNK, these characters and their associated DLC costumes must be re-acquired or unlocked via specific keys in the Last Round version. Most other standard DLC costumes will transfer without issue as long as you own the character unlock key in the new version.

### Can I play against friends who are still on the original Dead or Alive 6?
Unfortunately, there is no crossplay between the original Dead or Alive 6 and the Dead or Alive 6 Last Round version. To play with friends, everyone must be on the same version of the game. This is due to the underlying engine updates and balance changes present in the Last Round edition that make the two versions incompatible for synchronized play.

### What happens if I already started playing Last Round before transferring my data?
If you have already created a save file in Last Round, the game will not automatically ask you to transfer your old DOA6 data. To fix this, you must go into your console or PC storage settings and delete the existing Dead or Alive 6 Last Round save data. Once the system detects no save file, it will prompt you to transfer your legacy data the next time you reach the title screen.

### Is the upgrade available for the Core Fighters version?
Yes, players using the free-to-play Core Fighters version of Dead or Alive 6 can also take advantage of the data carry-over features. Any costumes or characters you purchased in the Core Fighters version of the original game can be migrated to the Last Round version following the same steps as the standard edition owners.
