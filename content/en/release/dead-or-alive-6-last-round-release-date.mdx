## Save Data and DLC Transfer

A common concern among the community regarding the **dead or alive 6 last round release date** was whether their previous investments would be honored. Team NINJA has confirmed a robust transfer system. Players can move their saved data and previously purchased DLC from the original version of Dead or Alive 6 to the Last Round edition within the same console family (e.g., PS4 to PS5 or Xbox One to Xbox Series X|S).

To facilitate this, the official website has provided a step-by-step guide. Generally, players will need to upload their save data to the cloud in the original game and then download it via the Last Round menu. It is important to note that cross-platform transfers (such as moving from PlayStation to Steam) are currently not supported.

### How to Transfer Your Progress
1.  Ensure your original Dead or Alive 6 game is updated to the latest version.
2.  Navigate to the "Options" menu and select "Save Data Upload."
3.  Launch Dead or Alive 6 Last Round on your new console.
4.  Select "Download Save Data" from the main menu.
5.  Restart the game to see your unlocked costumes and player rank.
