## Understanding the Upgrade Path for Physical and Digital Owners

The transition from the standard edition of Dead or Alive 6 to the Last Round iteration requires a clear understanding of your platform's ecosystem. Because physical discs act as security keys for your licenses, upgrading requires a few extra steps compared to purely digital copies.

According to player experiences, the process varies significantly depending on whether you are staying within the same console family. The game does not support cross-platform save transfers; for instance, you cannot move progress from a PlayStation 4 disc to an Xbox Series X|S console. However, if you stay within the PlayStation or Xbox ecosystem, your digital entitlements and save files can be successfully migrated.

## Platform-Specific DLC Transfer Steps

Transferring your purchased costumes and character unlocks requires an active internet connection and the same account used to purchase the content. The steps differ slightly between PlayStation, Xbox, and PC, so it is important to follow the correct sequence for your specific hardware.

### PlayStation 5 Migration Steps

If you are moving your content to a PlayStation 5, you must first ensure your PlayStation 4 version of the game is fully updated. The system requires the original game to be updated to Version 1.23 to enable the data export feature. 

1. Insert your PS4 disc or download your digital version of the game on your console.
2. Install the latest update (Ver. 1.23).
3. Sign in to your PlayStation Network account.
4. Launch the original game, navigate to the Main Menu, and select the option to upload your DLC data.
5. Launch the Last Round application while signed into the same PSN account. The game will automatically detect and link your uploaded DLC.

If you no longer have access to your original **dead or alive 6 last round physical copy** disc, you can download the free-to-play *Core Fighters* version from the PlayStation Store. This free application can read your account's DLC licenses and upload them to the cloud for transfer to the upgraded version.

### Xbox Series X|S Migration Steps

The Xbox ecosystem handles DLC licenses through its smart delivery and account management systems. Unlike PlayStation, Xbox players must manually install their original DLC packages before launching the upgraded game.

* Sign in to the Xbox account containing your purchases.
* Install your original Dead or Alive 6 DLC files onto your Xbox Series X|S console.
* Launch the Last Round application.
* The game will automatically scan your storage for installed DLC and apply it to your profile.

Whether you started with a digital license or a **dead or alive 6 last round physical copy**, the Xbox Network will verify your ownership as long as you use the exact same profile.

### Steam (PC) Migration Steps

PC players enjoy the most straightforward DLC transition. Because all purchases are tied directly to your Steam account, launching the Last Round application on the same Steam account will automatically transfer all eligible DLC costumes and characters.

## Step-by-Step Save Data Migration Guide

Moving your game progression, story mode completion, and unlocked gallery items is handled separately from your DLC licenses. The game is designed to prompt you to transfer save data upon its very first launch, ensuring your **dead or alive 6 last round physical copy** progression isn't lost.

### PlayStation 5 Save Data Transfer

PlayStation players can transfer their save files using three distinct methods depending on their hardware setup.

* **Same Console Transfer:** If you have both versions installed on the same PS5, the game will automatically scan the console's internal storage for existing save data upon launch.
* **Cloud Storage Transfer:** If you are moving to a new PS5 console, upload your PS4 save data to the PlayStation Plus Cloud Storage, download it to the new console, and then launch Last Round.
* **USB Storage Transfer (PS4 to PS5 only):** You can copy your PS4 save file to a USB drive, copy it to the PS5, and launch the game. Note that you cannot transfer data from a PS5 to another PS5 via USB storage.

### Xbox Series X|S Save Data Transfer

Xbox utilizes the cloud-based Xbox Network to keep save files synchronized. Ensure your console is connected to the internet, sign in to your profile, and launch Last Round. The game will retrieve your cloud save automatically and prompt you to import it.

### Steam (PC) Save Data Transfer

PC players can migrate their saves either through the Steam Cloud or by manually moving files. To transfer manually, copy the save folder from the original directory:

`\Users\(Your Username)\Documents\KoeiTecmo\DEADORALIVE6`

Paste this folder into the corresponding directory on your new PC before launching the game.

| **Xbox Series X|S** | Auto-detect / Cloud Sync | Not supported | Xbox Network Cloud |

## What Carries Over (and What Gets Left Behind)

While Team Ninja made an effort to support players upgrading their games, not all content is eligible for transfer. Licensing restrictions, engine changes, and competitive balance adjustments mean certain items must be re-acquired or purchased again.

### DLC and Costume Carry-Over Rules

Most standard costumes, hairstyles, and accessories will successfully carry over to your new game. However, collaboration content is a major exception. Due to expired licensing agreements, DLC costumes featuring guest characters like Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond from the *King of Fighters* series cannot be carried over.

### Progression and User Data

Your offline progression, including your story mode completion, DOA Quest progress, tutorials, and unlocked encyclopedia trivia, will transfer seamlessly. However, your online competitive data—such as your multiplayer ranking, ranking points, fighter level, and fight records—will be completely reset. This ensures a level playing field for the updated release.

To verify what will copy over to your new game, consult the detailed compatibility tables below.

| **Tutorial & Training Data** | Yes | Command, combo, and free training records remain. |
| **Encyclopedia & Trivia** | Yes | Unlocked lore entries are preserved. |

Certain items are permanently locked to your original platform and cannot be migrated under any circumstances.

## Troubleshooting Common Migration Issues

The transfer process is usually straightforward, but players occasionally run into errors where their save data or purchased DLC fails to appear. Understanding how to resolve these issues can save you time and prevent accidental data loss.

One common issue is the save transfer prompt not appearing when launching the game. This typically happens if you have already created a new save file on the Last Round version. The game will not overwrite existing save data. To fix this, you must go to your console's storage settings, delete any newly created Last Round save data, and relaunch the game to trigger the import prompt.

Additionally, community reports suggest that license restoration can resolve missing DLC issues. If your purchased outfits do not show up, navigate to your account settings on your console and select "Restore Licenses." This forces the console to re-verify all digital entitlements associated with your profile.

Finally, keep in mind that there is no crossplay support between the original game and the Last Round version. If you want to play with friends, you must all be on the same version of the game.

For more details on product specifications and updates, you can visit the [official Team Ninja product information page](https://teamninja-studio.com/doa6/lastround/us/productinfo/).

| Common Issue | Potential Cause | Recommended Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Save transfer prompt does not appear | Existing save file already present | Delete the new save file from console storage and restart. |
| Purchased DLC is missing in-game | Unrestored licenses or missing install | Restore licenses in console settings or manually install DLC. |
| Compilation characters locked | Missing character unlock keys | Purchase or redownload the individual character unlock keys. |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What should I do if I lost my dead or alive 6 last round physical copy?
If you no longer have access to your original disc, you can download the free-to-play *Core Fighters* edition of the game from your console's store. This version can access your account's saved data and DLC licenses, allowing you to upload them to the cloud for transfer to the upgraded version without needing the physical disc.

### Can I transfer my save data from the upgraded version back to the standard version?
No, save data transfers are strictly one-way. Once you migrate your progress to the upgraded version, you cannot transfer that updated progress back to the legacy version of the game.

### Why didn't my Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond costumes transfer over?
Due to licensing agreements with SNK, collaboration characters and their associated costumes cannot be carried over to the updated version of the game. You must purchase their respective unlock keys separately if they are available on the new platform.

### Is there crossplay between the standard edition and Last Round?
No, there is no crossplay support between the original release and the Last Round edition. Players must be running the same version of the game on the same platform family to play online together.
