While DLC unlocks your purchased outfits, your save data contains your unlocked in-game costumes, hairstyles, and story progression. Transferring your save data is a one-time process that occurs when you launch the game for the first time.

*Warning: You cannot transfer save data if you have already created a save file for the new edition. If you have already started playing, you must delete your new save data from your system storage to trigger the transfer prompt.*

### Local and Cloud Save Migration Methods

Depending on your platform, you can migrate your save files using local storage or cloud services.

If you are managing a **dead or alive 6 last round costume unlock** when using the free-to-play edition, there are unique rules to keep in mind. The free-to-play *Core Fighters* version only includes four playable characters: Kasumi, Marie Rose, Honoka, and NiCO.

For guest characters like Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond, separate unlock keys are always required, and their specific DLC outfits must be re-purchased due to licensing differences.

## Troubleshooting Common Costume Unlock Issues

Many players report issues where their transferred costumes do not appear in the closet. If your game fails to register your content, check the following troubleshooting steps:

*   **Check Character Licenses:** Ensure you own the Character Unlock Key for the fighter whose costume you are trying to access.
*   **Verify Game Updates:** On PlayStation, confirm that your base game was updated to Ver. 1.23 before you performed the upload.
*   **Reinstall DLC Packs:** On Xbox, verify that the individual DLC files are actively installed on your console's hard drive.
*   **Clear Duplicate Saves:** If the transfer prompt did not appear, delete your current save data for the new edition and restart the game.
*   **Check Region Compatibility:** Save data and DLC cannot be transferred across different regional accounts (e.g., transferring a Japanese region save to a North American game edition).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I perform a dead or alive 6 last round costume unlock if I upgraded from the standard edition?
To unlock your costumes, you must transfer your save data and DLC. On PlayStation, update the original game to Ver. 1.23, upload your DLC from the main menu, and then launch the new edition. On Xbox and Steam, ensure you are signed into the same account, install your DLC, and launch the game to trigger the automatic transfer.

### Can I transfer my costumes from the new edition back to the original game?
No. Content transfer is strictly a one-way street. Any save progression, items, or DLC purchased specifically for the newer edition cannot be migrated back to the original version of the game.

### Does the dead or alive 6 last round costume unlock transfer collaboration outfits?
No, collaboration costumes do not carry over. Specifically, DLC outfits and character unlock keys for Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond from the SNK franchise cannot be transferred due to licensing restrictions. You will need to purchase these characters and their respective outfits separately.

### What should I do if my save data transfer prompt didn't appear?
If the transfer prompt does not appear when you reach the title screen, it means the game has already created a new save file. To fix this, go to your system storage settings, delete the save data for the new edition, and restart the game. The system will then detect your old save file and prompt you to import it.
