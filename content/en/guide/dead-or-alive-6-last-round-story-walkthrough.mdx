## Tips for 100% Story Completion

To truly master the **dead or alive 6 last round story walkthrough**, you cannot simply rush to the end. The grid system requires you to fulfill certain conditions to unlock "Hidden Nodes." These often involve completing a match with a specific health percentage or performing a certain number of "Fatal Rushes" during a story fight.

*   **Watch Every Cutscene:** Some nodes only unlock after the preceding cinematic has played in its entirety.
*   **Check Character Levels:** Certain side stories are gated behind character-specific proficiency levels.
*   **Utilize Training Mode:** If a story fight is too difficult, jump into the command training for that character to learn their most effective "Bread and Butter" (BnB) combos.
*   **The Photo Mode Advantage:** The new Photo Mode in Last Round allows you to pause the action and study character hitboxes and animations, which can be a secret weapon for learning how to counter difficult AI opponents.

According to [official Team NINJA updates](https://teamninja-studio.com/doa6/lastround/us/), the Last Round edition has been specifically tuned for the latest hardware, meaning load times between story chapters are virtually non-existent on PS5 and Xbox Series X.
